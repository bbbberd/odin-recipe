Ingredient:
  • any veggies that raw and edible
  • any veggies that boiled
  • peanuts
  • coconut oil
  • coconut sugar
  • salt
  • garlic
  • chili(optional)
    Recipe:
  1. step1: fry peanuts with coconut oil
  2. step2: make the peanut sauce by smashing/blending garlic, salt, coconut sugar, chili(optional) and fried peanuts together
  3. step3: boil the veggies then put into bowl and add the raw veggies too
  4. step4: add the peanut sauce to the bowl and mix it with veggies
  5. step5: garnish all you like, add any thing you like (some protein or cracker)
