Ingredient
  • Fruits, all kind, ripe/unripe but edible (tropical are better)
  • coconut oil
  • peanuts
  • chili
  • salt
  • coconut sugar
    Recipe

  2. ground the peanuts with salt, coconut sugar, and chili(optional)

  4. add them the sauce or garnish it separated
